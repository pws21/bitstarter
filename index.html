The idea is to make site which will help customers decrease time and money they spent on searching photographers and video producers. You just need make a request and describe what kind of service you need and how much you pay.
Your request will be published on site and interested photographers start reply on it. They can reply until you will not close request and choose best of them for the job.
Each photographer will have a rating and reviews leaved by customers, so you'll see who is proffessional and who is novice. Photographers also can upload portfolio and leave links to Flickr and social network account.

Suppose that you have only 50 bucks and you need to make a photo session. What would you do in old maner?
You googling for photographers in your area and start calling them one by one:

- Are you available tomorrow?
- No
- I can spent only 50 dollars
- No. I want 100 for this work

So on...
Or you can just make a request like this:

I need a photo session near Orlando, FL on March 23. I'll pay 50 dollars for 2 hours.

and waiting for proposals...
